Site Name: Travel and Food in Guatemala

Site Purpose:

The goal of this website is to promote tourism and celebrate Guatemalan culture by showing its amazing places and delicious cuisine. It will inspire visitors to explore Guatemala and appreciate its beauty and traditions.

Scenarios:

  • What is the history and the culture in Guatemala?
  • What are the five best places in Guatemala?
  • What are the five best foods in Guatemala?
